Birches Valley

Birches Valley is a great place to visit. It is perfect for a family day out as there is a lot to do, such as The Gruffalo Trail and there is a park on site. There is also a large clearing where you can have a family picnic. The clearing is also used for festivals.

There is also Go Ape and a cycle center where you can rent a bike to go on one of the bike trails.
