Museum of Cannock Chase

The museum has a lot to offer for all ages. The museum has a Miners Cottage Gallery and a Coal Mining Gallery. The museum is both fun and educational as it has an interactive toy gallery where you can not only look at the old toys but also play with them. The museum also has a gift shop and cafe. The shop offers not only toys but history books, which go into more detail about the museums displays.

The museum goes into great depth about what it was like living like in that time. The museum has visual representations of this with readable information and audio information, providing information on each different part. Outside the museum is one of the wheels the used at the mines.
